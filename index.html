      Features

      Fixture / event ticketing

      • Sell season tickets, fixture and event tickets online 24/7 to increase attendances
      • Launch digital ticketing in minutes using Fanbase for your fixtures and club events. Our platform is exclusively built to solve the challenges sports teams face, making it the best sports ticketing solution available

      Manage hospitality

      • Increase hospitality sales and revenue while saving time for club staff responsible for bookings
      • Collect key information, send automated emails and check attendees off via your digital guest list each week

      Marketing and fan data

      • Track your fan data and identify audiences for cross-selling, upselling and sponsorship opportunities
      • Use your Fan CRM to get a 360-degree view of your fans and their engagement with your club
      • Use this data to power your marketing strategy and commercial growth

      Memberships (coming soon)

      • Create flexible memberships, with automated benefits that anyone around the world can enjoy
      • Sell digital memberships to your fans, manage membership communications and remove admin involved in honouring membership benefits

      Website, iOS, Android

      • Make it easy for your fans to engage with your club across mobile and web
      • By integrating with your website and deploying an app experience for your club, we can make it delightful for fans to engage with your club across digital channels
